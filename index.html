High Quality Hand-Crafted Woodworking

Milanwood Ltd. | Est. 2012

Milanwood Ltd. was established in 2012 and since then has been making custom furniture for individuals and corporate entities. All designs are hand-crafted and tailored to the customers specific desires to ensure the best combination of quality, durability and asthectics at a reasonable price.


Milanwood is endlessly delighted and increasingly fascinated by the joy of selling beautifully made and enduring pieces of furniture to everyone.


In the background I (Milan Pistek, Founder & CEO) have been in the custom woodworking industry for about 34 years. Working for architectural millwork companies as lead woodworker specializing for commercial/industrial residential projects throughout Canada & USA. Including special architectural millwork and furniture for courthouses,churches,commercial/industrial buildings, yachts and luxury residential houses.
